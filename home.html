About Me

I am currently a PhD student in computer science at the University of Montreal under the supervision of Professor Houari Sahraoui. I am a member of the GEODES Software Engineering Research Group. I graduated from the National School of Computer Science (Tunisia) as a Computer Engineer in 2010. I also obtained my Master of Science in Software Engineering and Business Intelligence from the same school in 2011. My research interests include the application of artificial intelligence techniques to software engineering and the combinations of static and dynamic program analysis techniques for program comprehension.
